About BPC Strong

For over 25 years Bystol Performance Center has been providing the best personal training experience in the Chicagoland area. We have serviced clientele from professional athletes to those just getting started on their fitness journey.

Our team has extensive knowledge in the fields of exercise science, movement as medicine, and corrective exercise. Driven by a relentless passion for results, Bystol Performance Center will educate, empower, and encourage you to achieve all of your health and fitness goals. Regardless of whether that goal is to sculpt the body of your dreams, improve your golf or tennis game, or you simply want to adopt a healthier lifestyle, BPC is the place for you !
